Rock-Paper-Scissors

How to Play?

In this game, the computer will be one player.
The user will choose one of the three choices (Rock, Paper, Scissors).
The computer will keep track of the choice and try to defeat its opponent.
Rules:
  • Each player will choose one of Rock, Paper, or Scissors.
  • Both players will not be aware of the other's choice.
  • The winner is decided by a set of rules:
  • Rock blunts scissors.
  • Paper covers rock.
  • Scissors cut paper.
  • If both players choose the same thing, the game will be a draw.
